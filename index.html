About the Shop

Neighborhood craft, sharp results

SD Barber Shop is a neighborhood barbershop on Clairemont Mesa Blvd offering men's haircuts, beard trims, head shaves, and hot towel shaves. Walk-ins are welcome, the shop is wheelchair accessible, and credit cards are accepted.

Open six days a week (closed Wednesdays), the shop has built a solid local following, friendly, no-frills service focused on clean fades and classic cuts, with specialty experience in Asian hair textures.

Good To Know

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment?
No, walk-ins are welcome. Stop by during open hours and get in the chair; no booking required.
What forms of payment do you accept?
Credit cards are accepted at the shop, in addition to standard payment methods.
Is the shop accessible if I use a wheelchair?
Yes, the shop is wheelchair accessible.
Do you cut Asian hair textures?
Yes, the shop has focused experience cutting and styling Asian hair textures and patterns, in addition to standard fades, tapers, and classic cuts.
What are your hours?
Monday, Tuesday, and Thursday through Saturday, 10:00 AM, 6:00 PM. Sunday, 10:00 AM, 4:00 PM. Closed Wednesdays. Hours can shift on holidays, so it's worth calling ahead at (858) 279-8199.
